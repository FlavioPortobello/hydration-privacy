💧 Hydration App

Privacy Policy

👋 Introduction

Welcome to the Hydration App ("the App"), a personal health tool that calculates your daily fluid intake needs based on data from your WHOOP wearable, local weather conditions, and your body metrics.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to full transparency.

📊 Data We Access

When you connect your WHOOP account, the App requests access to the following data via the official WHOOP API:

  • Workout data – strain score and sweat loss estimates
  • Sleep data – sleep duration and sleep debt
  • Recovery data – recovery score
  • Profile data – used only to identify your account

Additionally, the App may use:

  • Location data – only to fetch local weather/temperature (not stored)
  • Body weight – entered manually by you, stored locally on your device only

🔒 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to calculate your personalized daily hydration goal and reminder schedule. Specifically:

  • WHOOP data is fetched in real time and used for calculation only
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

All processing happens locally on your device.

🔗 WHOOP Integration

This App integrates with WHOOP via the official WHOOP Developer API. Authentication is handled through WHOOP's OAuth 2.0 system. We never see or store your WHOOP password.

You can revoke the App's access to your WHOOP data at any time through your WHOOP account settings.

📱 Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your body weight setting
  • Your wake and sleep time preferences
  • Your daily water consumption log (current day only)
  • WHOOP access token (encrypted, stored securely on device)

None of this data is transmitted to any server operated by the App developer.

🧒 Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

✏️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your WHOOP account at any time
  • Delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App
  • Request information about what data is stored
